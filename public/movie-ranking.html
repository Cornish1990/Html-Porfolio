Top Movies According To Bobby

3 All Time favourite Films

The Shawshank Redemption

It is a compelling drama, and a very moving film, perfectly written, acted and shot.

Wedding Crashes

A simply hilarious comedy that will have you crying with laughter from start to finish.

Lord of the rings

Tolkien is superb at building tension and delivering epic speeches but seems disinclined to dwell on the horrors of warfare.
